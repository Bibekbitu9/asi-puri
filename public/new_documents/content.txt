Founded over a century and a half ago, the ASI regulates all archaeological activities, manages site museums, and maintains ancient monuments and archaeological remains of national importance across the country. </p><ul><li>About Puri Circle</li></ul><p>The Puri Circle was officially established on November 9, 2022, following the bifurcation of the erstwhile Bhubaneswar Circle. Our core mission is to carry out scientific conservation, preservation, exploration, excavation, and structural beautification of regional treasures. By focusing dedicated technical and administrative expertise on coastal, western, and northern Odisha, we ensure these irreplaceable historic sites are preserved to world-class standards while expanding public outreach and education. </p><ul><li>Jurisdiction &amp; Organizational Structure</li></ul><p>Headed by the Superintending Archaeologist, the Puri Circle exercises administrative control over <strong>40 centrally protected monuments and sites</strong> spread across <strong>15 districts of Odisha</strong>. This expansive zone extends up to the borders of West Chhattisgarh, North Jharkhand, and North-East West Bengal, bounded to the east by the Bay of Bengal. </p><ul><li>Office Information</li></ul><p><strong>Monuments</strong></p><ul><li>Monument Information</li><li>Historical Details</li><li>Architectural Features</li><li>Visitor Information</li><li>Image Gallery</li></ul><p><strong>Virtual Walkthroughs</strong></p><ul><li>Interactive 360° Experiences</li><li>Full-Screen Viewing</li><li>Mobile Compatibility</li><li>Future VR Integration Support</li></ul><p><strong>Events &amp; Activities</strong></p><ul><li>Upcoming Events</li><li>Workshops</li><li>Heritage Awareness Programmes</li><li>Exhibitions</li><li>Cultural Activities</li><li>Five-Year Event Archive with Photos, Videos and Reports</li></ul><p><strong>Conservation</strong></p><p>The core mandate of the Puri Circle is the material and structural preservation of monuments using non-destructive, scientifically backed methodologies.</p><ul><li><strong>Ongoing &amp; Completed Projects:</strong> Track our active conservation campaigns—ranging from the delicate chemical cleaning of moss-laden stone surfaces to major structural grouting, underpinning foundations, and watertightening ancient roofs against monsoonal rains.</li><li><strong>Conservation Documentation:</strong> Access detailed case studies, architectural drawings, and before-and-after photographic records that document the survival and restoration of crumbling stone marvels.</li><li>Ongoing Projects</li><li>Completed Projects</li><li>Conservation Documentation</li></ul><p><strong>Excavation &amp; Exploration</strong></p><p>Unearthing the buried narratives of the past.
