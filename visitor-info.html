E-Ticket & Booking

For convenience and a contactless experience, visitors are highly encouraged to book entry tickets online in advance. E-tickets help save time at entrance gates, especially during weekends and national holidays.

Book Tickets Online 🎫

Do's and Don'ts

  • Do: Preserve cleanliness and dispose of waste only in trash bins.
  • Do: Cooperation with security staff and guides is essential.
  • Do: Hire only certified ASI guides for historical tours.
  • Don't: Do not touch, sit on, write on, or climb monument carvings.
  • Don't: Polythene bags and plastic packaging are prohibited inside monument premises.
  • Don't: Videography or drone photography is strictly prohibited without prior permission from the Circle Office.

ASI Puri Circle

The Puri Circle of the Archaeological Survey of India is dedicated to preserving the rich archaeological and cultural heritage of Odisha.
